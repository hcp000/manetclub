Here,you are going to open the gate to the musical world……

With a elegant design, the concert hall of Manet Meadow International Club can provide you with professional acoustic facilities. Private concert halls can fulfill vocal music learning requirements, which gives full expression of the delicacy of refined musical arts. Besides, a variety of chamber concerts and music salons will be held periodically in the concer hall for auditory pleasure of the members.

The concert hall covers an area of 214㎡(23m*12m*3.5m). The layout and capacity are listed below: Theatre --120 persons; Reception--120 persons; Banquet--100 persons; Classroom--80 persons; Board--60 persons; Hollow Square--60 persons; U-shape--60 persons.

Manet Meadow Gallery is a super-unique visual art exhibition center located in the western suburbs of Beijing. The massive display panels are made from century-old ship wood found in the depth of South China Sea. This gallery serves as a holy land of contemporary art for art lovers and collectors. Here, an artistic feast is waiting for you……

The gallery covers 1200㎡. It is a super-unique visual art exhibition center located in the western suburbs of Beijing. The artistic value of the exhibits have won approval of China National Academy of Painting.To promote cultural-exchange, the gallery has a fantastic art collection of various schools from both home and abroad. In addition, it will invite famous artists to give lectures on art and hold academic salons on regular basis for club members.

Opening Hours：10:00am--22:00pm

In the vaulted library, you may enjoy the great accompany of books.

The vaulted library is located at the center of the club. The hand-painted Impressionism works on the vault generates super visual effect. The 460 ㎡ library is equipped with two art-themed studies, where you can mute the hustle and bustle, and enjoy your own tranquility.Here club members can browse through a fantastic collection of books with traditional British afternoon tea and the aroma of coffee. Meanwhile,it holds the brand salon"Urban Thinkers" on a regular basis.
