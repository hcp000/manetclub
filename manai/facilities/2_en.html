Featuring its graceful glass French windows and 80,000 square meters of exclusive grassland outside, Xiang Hui Chinese Restaurant consists of a main hall and 19 themed salon halls. Feng Qiming, a renowned head-chef from Hong Kong cook the specialties in person. He specialize in new Cantonese cuisine, Shandong cuisine and Hunan cuisine. It is the ideal restaurant for private banquets and family gatherings.

The dining hall covers an area of 190㎡(16.4m*11.6m*3m). The layout and capacity are listed below: Banquet--80 persons;Theatre --60 persons; Reception --60 persons; Board--60 persons; Classroom--40 persons; Hollow Square--40 persons; U-shape--30 persons.
