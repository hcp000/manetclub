The unique skyline makes the garden in the courtyard look exquisite and elegant. It is an ideal spot for receptions,mini-conferences, small evening parties and various kinds of themed outdoor activities.

Surrounded with green willows, the yard lies on a gentle slope by the river. This is the most suitable places for a variety of tasteful outdoor activities and BBQ theme parties.

The yard covers an area of 416㎡(16m*26m). The layout and capacity are listed below: Reception--120 persons; Theatre --100 persons; Banquet--80 persons; Classroom--60 persons; Board--60 persons; Hollow Square--60 persons; U-shape--60 persons.

Green meadow, gentle slopes, forest, away from the hustle and bustle of metropolis, here you can embrace the nature with your whole heart. Bask in the warm sunshine; hear the birdlings twitter and cicadas sing. You slow down your step and take a deep breath. The sweetness of the air and the fragrance of the plants make u cast aside all your worries. You can't help opening your heart to such special greenness and serenity. Here you can hold various theme weddings, large conferences and themed outdoor activities.
