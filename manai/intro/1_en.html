Manet Club

In constant pursuit of refined art and classic lifestyle, the first art themed club in Beijing--Manet Meadow International Club--opened in 2009. People can join the club and become members if invited.

Manet Meadow International Club is located in the western suburbs of Beijing, next to Fragrant Hills, a world-famous historical resort. Away from the hustle and bustle of big metropolis, it is adjacent to Jade Spring Hill and the Summer Palace, renowned as "Imperial Garden". Meanwhile, China's financial center and administration center, Zhongguancun High-tech Park, Tsinghua University and Peking University are all situated near at hand. With superior landscape, this is the place of human wonders and natural treasures.

Manet Meadow International Club owns 80,000 square meters of forest and grassland exclusively. It is the leading art-themed club in Beijing. The name of the club originates from the masterpiece Le déjeuner sur l'herbe (Luncheon on the Grass) of Édouard Manet, a French virtuoso of Impressionism. Featuring an academism design, the complex covers 21,000 square meters, in which Manet Meadow Gallery, Concert Hall and Vaulted Library are located. It provides you special artistic atmosphere and a variety of leisure facilities, including Chinese restaurant with art-themed salons, Western restaurant, banquets and meetings, garden view suites, fitness center, Lotus Spa and children recreational center. The thoughtful customized private service will satisfy all your needs and make you feel right at home.
