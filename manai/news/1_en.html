Manet Meadow Gallery is the only art exhibition center in China which is established in high-end club. Meanwhile, it's also the only display center authorized by China National Academy of Painting which is located in Beijing. The gallery host a variety of top-class themed art exhibitions and academic salons periodically, which perfectly combines life with arts hand humanities. It helps cultivate your taste in arts.

Manet Meadow Concert is the annual musical feast of the club. The club has hosted the concert successfully for three years. The concert is gradually becoming the best outdoor concert in China which shows highest-profile. The most internationally influential musicians are invited to perform their best pieces for you on the Manet Meadow. In a quiet night, your heart will be truly touched by the graceful melody. This is a wonderful chance for you to embrace mother nature as well.

Wiener Phiharmoniker Cruise is one of the most classic trips well-selected for the members of Manet Meadow International Club. On the deluxe cruise, you may witness the rehearsals of two world-famous Phiharmonikers, Wiener Phiharmoniker and Berliner Phiharmoniker. You may listen to supreme live concerts and have joyful conversation with musical virtuosos in the banquet. Close your eyes, you are going to step on the fairyland of European musical art.

With humourous lectures, Zhou Haihong, the most influential musical educationalist in China, brings you to the wonderland of music. He will combines the beauty of music and the beauty of life, makeing you fully immersed in the splendour of melodies.

Zhang Hongyan, the Lute Queen, is the most influential contemporary lute performer and educationalist. She is a professor and master supervisor in China Conservatory of Music. She is considered as one of the most outstanding performer and educationalist in contemporary Chinese and World's musical world. Here, with her ingenious lute skill"whisper while playing", she gently stirs the strings and unveils the legendary world of lute music.

This is the stage for children who love music and have musical talents. Here, children can give performance and learn from each other, which strengthens their interests in music. With the tutelage of musical masters, children will truly learn to appreciate the essence of musical art and understand the happiness that music brings to them. This place nurtures musical masters for the future.

  • The Night for Urban Thinkers
  • Woman Salon

"Urban Thinkers" is one of the most important salon brands of the club. The core concept of Urban Thinkers is to converse with the future, the world and the soul. It strives to establish a communication platform for elites form all walks of life. Hosted by BOA of the club, it invites those people who have made outstanding contributions to the development of the society to discuss hot issues, such as how to establish a harmonious society or city. It is destined to become the origin place of discussions on society and urban development.

Participated and hosted by Zhang Yue, a famous hostess from CCTV, the salon focuses on successful women and holds talks on the topics such as family, daily life and career. It cares for every female member and their families, enlightening them with thinking. It offers diversified perspectives to female members. By sharing opinions with their families, those members become more rational, thus completing the transcendence toward fitness, confidence and wisdom.
