Rights of Corporate Membership

• Corporate nominees and any immediate family member, such as spouse and any children under 21 years of age shall be entitled to full rights of individual membership provided by Manet Meadow International Club.

• Corporate nominees and any immediate family member, such as spouse and any children under 21 years of age shall be entitled to free healthy breakfast in the designated restaurant.

• Any corporate nominee shall be invited to various forums and salons hosted by Manet Meadow International Club.

• Any corporate nominee shall be entitled to the following facilities free of charge by the Club:Swimming pool/Fitness Center/Table Tennis Room/ Library

• When using the activity sites in the Club, corporate nominees shall be entitled to favorable prices.

• A corporate membership can not be transferred to an individual membership. It can not be inherited.

• A corporate membership may be transferred after three years if the membership transfer is approved by the Club.(For more information, please refer to Membership Manual)

• Corporate nominees shall be entitled to the membership of any club domestic and overseas which is associated online with Manet Meadow International Club.
